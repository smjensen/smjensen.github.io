Target Audience

As stated on our homepage, anyone who lives in Eastern Idaho knows that just because the weather looks clear when you start a journey does not mean that you will be traveling in livable, non dangerous conditions five minutes after beginning their journey. If these citizens had a weather map, they could see what their journey would include and plan accordingly.
