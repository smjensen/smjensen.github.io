Trip Advisor

First, choose a method, and we will recommend a route for you.

Kayaking

This will be on the North Fork. It requires a very low experience level. Anyone can Kayak and enjoy the journey.

Paddle Boarding

This is on one of Idaho's pristine lakes. The skill level is somewhat high. Without a good sense of balance, expect to get wet.

Water Rafting

This is of course, the main attraction. You can choose any river included in the program, and whether to go with a guide or not. Skill required may vary.

Water Tubing

Choose a lazy winding course and drift all day if you like. No skill required.
