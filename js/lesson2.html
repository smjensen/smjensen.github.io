Design Principles Lesson 2

Rule of Thirds

Ford Motor Co.
https://www.ford.com/


Ford must have some good photographers because they give an excellent example of the rule of thirds. Say what you want about the quality of their cars, this demonstrates that a photo with a specific amount of empty space next to the object in question (in this case, a car) will draw even more attention to the object.

Contrast

Mastercard
https://www.mastercard.us/en-us.html


Mastercard's logo is a perfct example of contrast. Most of the time, contrast highlights the simplicity of design elements, and mastercard is no exception. The black background with the simple two tones of the designes crossing instantly makes anyone who knows of mastercard to think of the product in question.

Visual Hierarchy

Walmart
https://www.walmart.com/


A visual hierarchy is designed to help the user see a large amount of information in a short period of time. Because Walmart wants users to buy their products, they are a great example of a company that is working very hard to implement the visual hierarchy to carry our eyes across all of their products.
