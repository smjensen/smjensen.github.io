Color Scheme

I initially chose my colors based off of a potential banner picture that I dug up. Things got altered a bit over time, but overall the colors have stayed fairly true. I stuck with colors that are liked typically by men and women and did a combination of soothing and energizing colors.

From that picture I wanted something contrasting, but that still fit for my main color.

I tend to feel like just solid white is too bright for my eyes on the computer so I picked a neutral off white instead. Something still bright, but not painful to look at or that will blind you in the dark.

I chose this one because it contrasts slightly with the background color and because it looks good on my home page and with the navy.
