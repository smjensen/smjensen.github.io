Heavenly Kisses

by H. Stratton

Nothing can brighen the drab monotony of the landscape better than pockets of wildflowers. They are a gift from the heavens as a reminder that God is always there to lift our hearts and lighten our load. The pioneers must have delighted at the sign of life from these beauties as they trudged along their well-beaten path. We can delight in their presence as well as we press forward on our own trail. Enjoying the journey is a necessary skill to refine. If we can't find happiness while on this earth, how will we expect to achieve it in the next?
